After analyzing the research findings, Hyundai Motor America is chosen as the best company for investment. Additionally, Hyundai plans to invest USD 26 billion in the U.S. over the next few years, enhancing its market share and innovation, especially in the electric vehicle sector. These attributes make it a solid investment opportunity. 

Ford Motor Company, despite showing a robust rebound in sales and focusing on EV technology, faces substantial risks, including rising commodity prices and labor costs, affecting its overall attractiveness as an investment. Stellantis N.V., on the other hand, is undergoing significant restructuring in an attempt to improve operational efficiency; however, this process poses its own risks and uncertainties, making it a less favorable choice compared to Hyundai. Thus, considering Hyundai’s ongoing sales growth and strategic investments, it stands out as the most suitable candidate for investment.
